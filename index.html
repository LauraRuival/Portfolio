About Me

I'm a full-stack software engineer with over 15 years of experience. I'm interested primarily in Web and mobile app development. I have used many languages for development projects including HTML, CSS, JavaScript, VB.Net, and C#. I've done mobile app development using using Swift and SwiftUI. I am also well-versed in relational database design and have worked primarily with SQL Server. I enjoy creating user-centered UIs and solving problems.

Since 2018 I have been teaching coding and robotics in the Alpine Union school district. I also teach computer science courses to high school students through UC San Diego Extended Studies Division. I currently teach iOS Swift Programming, Front End Web Technologies and Robotics with JavaScript. I also teach Scratch coding camps to elementary students for Sally Ride Science Academy.

In 2024 I joined the board of Alpine Education Foundation, a non-profit that provides STEAM education to elementary school students in the Alpine Union School District. I am proud to serve on the board as a director and member of the Grant Committee.
